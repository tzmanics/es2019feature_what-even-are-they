Array.prototype.flat

Michael Ficarra, Brian Terlson & Mathias Bynens

What even is it: an array method that "smooshes" down a multi-dimensional array by one factor or the specified depth.
let shoes = ["$300", ["runs small", "betch"], "mine"]; shoes.flat() //["$300", "runs small", "betch", "mine"];
